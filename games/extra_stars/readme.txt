Extra Stars - 5 reel, 3 row, 10 fixed paylines slot.

Symbols: cireasa, lamaie, portocala, pruna, struguri, lubenita, banane, septar, stea (wild).

Rules:
- 10 fixed paylines, wins evaluated left-to-right AND right-to-left.
- stea (wild) appears only on reels 2, 3, 4.
- When stea lands, the entire reel expands to wild, pays, then triggers a respin.
- Locked wild reels stay expanded during respins; other reels re-spin.
- Each new stea on reels 2/3/4 during a respin expands that reel and triggers another respin.

- Max win cap: 2000x (10 lines x bidirectional 5-kind septar)

Target RTP: 95.78% (reel strips require optimization tuning)
